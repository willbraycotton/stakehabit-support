The Service

StakeHabit is a habit-tracking app that lets you set financial penalties for missed habits. When you create a habit, you choose a stake amount (the penalty per miss) and a charity to receive the funds. If you log a missed check-in, your saved payment method is charged that amount.

StakeHabit is intended for personal accountability. You must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter into binding agreements to use the Service.

Payments and Charges

When you set up a habit with a financial stake, you authorize StakeHabit to charge your saved payment method each time you log a missed check-in for that habit.

  • You are only charged when you explicitly log a miss by tapping "No, charge me."
  • Charges are processed immediately through Stripe, our payment processor.
  • You will receive an email receipt for each charge.
  • Stake amounts are limited to $1–$20 per miss.

By saving a payment method and creating a habit, you acknowledge that real money will be charged upon confirmed misses and that these charges are subject to the no-refund policy below.

No Refunds

All charges are final and non-refundable. Once a charge is processed, it cannot be reversed.

If you believe you were charged in error — for example, you completed your habit but it was logged as a miss — contact us at willbraycotton@gmail.com within 7 days of the charge. We will investigate and, if the charge was our error, issue a correction at our discretion.

Charges resulting from your own confirmed missed check-ins are not eligible for refund under any circumstances.

Fees

StakeHabit deducts processing fees from the amount donated to charity. You are always charged exactly your stake amount — fees do not increase what you pay.

  • StakeHabit processing fee: 5% of the stake
  • Stripe payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

The remainder after fees is donated to your selected charity. A receipt showing the charge amount and donation amount will be sent to your receipt email address.

Donations

StakeHabit donates the net proceeds from penalty charges to the charity you selected. Donations are batched and sent within 72 hours of each charge. StakeHabit is not itself a charitable organization — donations are made on your behalf to registered third-party charities.

You will not receive a tax deduction receipt for these donations, as they are made on your behalf rather than directly by you.
