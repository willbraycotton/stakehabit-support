How it works

Simple, ruthless accountability.

Three steps. No complexity. Just a system that makes breaking your habit cost you something real.

1

Set the stake

Create your habit, set a penalty ($1–$20 per miss), and choose a charity you'd genuinely hate to fund. The more you dislike it, the better it works.

2

Check in daily

Open the app each day and log your result. Did it? Nothing happens — just your streak growing. Reminders keep you honest.

3

Miss and pay

Log a miss and your stake is immediately charged and donated. No grace periods, no excuses. The sting is the point.

Why it works

Loss aversion is real.

Research consistently shows people are more motivated to avoid losing something than to gain an equivalent reward. StakeHabit turns that psychology into your daily advantage.

Most habit apps rely on streaks and badges. Those are easy to ignore. A donation to a cause you oppose? That's not.
